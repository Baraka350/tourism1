About Lake Kivu

Lake Kivu is one of Africa's largest and most beautiful lakes, nestled between Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The lake is renowned for its stunning views, serene atmosphere, and a wealth of outdoor activities.

Things to Do Around Lake Kivu

  • Boat tours on the lake
  • Hiking around the surrounding hills
  • Visit the islands in the lake, including Amahoro and Idjwi Island
  • Explore the nearby Kibuye and Gisenyi towns

Attractions Near Lake Kivu

  • Volcanoes National Park (Gorilla trekking)
  • Nyungwe Forest (Chimpanzee tracking)
  • Explore local markets and crafts in Gisenyi
  • Visit the Congo Nile Trail
